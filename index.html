HbA1c Results Overview

The chart below illustrates the distribution of baseline HbA1c results for the 45 enrolled participants, providing a foundational understanding of their current blood sugar control.

HbA1c Breakdown (of 45 enrolled):

  • 28 participants (62%) had HbA1c ≥ 6.5% (indicating diabetes).
  • 8 participants (18%) had HbA1c between 5.7–6.4% (indicating prediabetes).
  • 9 participants (20%) had HbA1c < 5.7% (indicating normal blood sugar levels; ongoing monitoring is crucial as HbA1c levels can fluctuate).

All 45 participants were selected for enrollment based on a confirmed diabetes diagnosis through Medical Check-Ups (MCUs) or medical claims data.

Program Overview – DMP

The Diabetes Management Program (DMP) is a core offering of Loop Health, providing structured and continuous support to empower individuals in managing their diabetes effectively.

The DMP Includes:

  • 🩺
    Regular health coach video consultations for personalized guidance.
  • 📱
    Daily logging via the Loop app and glucometer-enabled blood sugar tracking for consistent monitoring.
  • 💬
    Personalized habit feedback, educational content, and wellness tips delivered through the app and WhatsApp.
  • 🌐
    WhatsApp follow-ups specifically designed to support those with limited app connectivity.

🎯

Program Goal

To improve blood sugar control through consistent digital care and personalized coaching, fostering sustainable health behaviors.

Program Enrollment & Follow-Up Plan
