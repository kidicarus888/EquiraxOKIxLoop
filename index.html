Program Enrollment & Follow-Up Plan

As of July 17, 2025, the initial enrollment phase is complete, and a clear plan is in place for ongoing monitoring and support to ensure long-term success and data validation.

Current Status:

  • 45 participants successfully onboarded into DMP during 19 June enrollment session in Palembang.
  • Baseline HbA1c testing for all attendees has been completed, providing crucial initial health markers.
  • 15 extra Loop Health Kits were left with OKI HR for future additional onboarding of identified employees unable to join on 19 June.

Follow-Up Plan:

  • Follow-up HbA1c testing is strategically planned for Month 6 and Month 12 to rigorously validate long-term clinical outcomes.
  • The program is designed to support day-to-day behavior change, fostering consistent healthy habits.
  • It also focuses on long-term outcome tracking, ensuring measurable improvements in employee health and well-being.
